# GARAGE F1 — image generation prompts

All assets use the `photorealistic-natural` use case and form one coherent editorial photo shoot: a modern independent auto repair workshop with charcoal walls, matte-black equipment, realistic steel and concrete textures, natural daylight and restrained red accents. Every image excludes text, logos, watermarks, readable plates, trademarks, racing imagery and staged stock-photo gestures.

## 1. Hero — `hero-premium-auto-service.webp`

- Intended placement: home page hero.
- Final format: WebP, 1672×941; mobile crop 800×1000.
- Layout requirement: car and mechanic on the right; dark negative space on the left for the headline and CTAs.

```text
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: automotive service website hero background
Primary request: a premium modern independent auto repair workshop in Batumi, Georgia, photographed as a coherent editorial automotive campaign. A clean dark graphite European sedan with no visible brand badge is parked inside a spacious service bay; one professional mechanic in simple charcoal workwear is naturally inspecting the open hood on the right side of the frame.
Scene/backdrop: contemporary real workshop, charcoal walls, matte black equipment, subtle restrained red safety accents, clean concrete floor, realistic tools, no luxury showroom staging.
Style/medium: ultra-photorealistic editorial automotive photography, natural textures, believable workshop details, no CGI look.
Composition/framing: 16:9 wide cinematic frame, eye-level 28mm lens. Keep the entire left 48% dark, calm and visually uncluttered as negative space for large white website copy and buttons. Place the car, open hood, mechanic and brightest highlights on the right half. The mobile center crop must still show part of the car and mechanic without cutting essential details.
Lighting/mood: soft overcast daylight entering from high side windows mixed with subtle practical workshop lights, controlled contrast, premium but authentic, confident and precise.
Color palette: deep graphite, black, neutral steel, muted concrete, restrained racing-red accents only.
Materials/textures: realistic painted metal, rubber, concrete, clean worn tools and natural fabric.
Constraints: no text, no logos, no watermarks, no readable license plate, no visible automotive trademarks, no racing cars, no helmets, no exaggerated sparks, no neon, no staged thumbs-up pose, no extra people, no clutter.
Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape.
```
